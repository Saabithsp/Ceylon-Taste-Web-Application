Cloves

Cloves are the aromatic dried unopened whole flower buds of the evergreen trees. Cloves are the pungent dried entire flower buds of evergreen plants. The plant was introduced to the nation by Arabian traders or Colonial rulers. Cloves are mostly used to flavour both sweet and savoury meals. Hand-picked, Cloves grade 1, and Cloves FAQ are the three main forms of cloves exported overseas. The quality and aroma of the cloves that we harvested from well-maintained local cultivations were preserved due to their authenticity.
