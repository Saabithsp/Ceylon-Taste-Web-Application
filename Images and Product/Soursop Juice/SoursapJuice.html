Soursap Juice

The soursop fruit is a tropical evergreen tree that grows between 5-20 meters tall. Its fruits are small green berries that have a sweet taste and are covered in tiny hairs. These hairs help protect the fruit from predators and insects. The fruit contains high levels of vitamin C and antioxidants, making it a great food for humans and animals alike.
